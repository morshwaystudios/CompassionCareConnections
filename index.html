About Us

Compassionate Care Connections is built on love, respect, and community strength. We are dedicated to being compassionate caregivers and deliver top-tier in-home care while empowering our clients to live independently, safely, and joyfully.

We believe health care isn’t just a service — it’s a human right, delivered with empathy and excellence. From medical assistance to wellness education, we serve every need with the same heart we’d show our own family.

CPR Training

Knowing CPR can mean the difference between life and death — and we believe everyone should be empowered with that ability. Our certified CPR & First Aid training is designed for individuals, families, caregivers, and community leaders.

These courses are interactive, hands-on, and led by experienced instructors who provide real-world scenarios to help you feel prepared. Upon completion, participants receive a valid BLS American Heart Association CPR certification recognized by national health authorities. Mobile Individual Classes Available upon request.

Whether you’re a parent, teacher, healthcare volunteer, or simply someone who wants to be ready, this training could make you someone’s hero.

Notary Services

Need important documents notarized? We’ve got you covered. Our professional notary public services are fast, secure, and provided with care and confidentiality.

We assist with a variety of documents including powers of attorney, healthcare directives, affidavits, deeds, and more. As part of our mission to serve, these services are available at a minimal or waived fee for community members in need.

Our goal is to reduce barriers and provide support wherever legal documentation meets life’s important decisions.
